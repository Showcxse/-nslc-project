OBJECTIVE

We have been recruited by Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport (ATL) to assist them in tracking the revenue from flying contracts during 2015. They requested that we account for costs like security delays and air system delays as well as income streams like gate rentals and airline delay fees on a day-by-day basis. Using this information, we are able to advise the airport on how to modify delay fees and gate expenses on a monthly basis to maintain and optimize profits that are relatively constant from month to month. By gathering information about consumer demographics and creating tables with data for an understandable explanation, we have accomplished the task set upon us.
